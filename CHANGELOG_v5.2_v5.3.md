# Betynz v5.3 — Launch Readiness + Community & Retention

This combined release includes the planned v5.2 and v5.3 work.

## Launch readiness
- Four-step first-run onboarding.
- Public system-health page with stale-data warnings.
- Notification centre and latest-snapshot alerts.
- Terms, Privacy Notice, local data export and account deletion controls.
- Guest access remains open; secure accounts sync saved features.
- PWA cache updated and notification clicks return to Betynz.

## Community and retention
- Follow any of the 18 engines.
- Favourite leagues and teams with dashboard shortcuts.
- Daily visit streak.
- Reactions and respectful comments in match-detail popups.
- Shareable 1080 × 1350 prediction cards generated in the browser.
- Transparent engine-performance page based only on settled records.
- Supabase migration with RLS for comments, reactions, follows and activity days.

## Safety
- No payment gates were enabled.
- No prediction-engine or workflow logic was changed.
- Community rules prohibit guaranteed-win claims, harassment and spam.
