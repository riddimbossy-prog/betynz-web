# PapaSense v1.7 Four-Engine System

## 1. Papa Primary — default

The best all-evidence direction. It blends venue, overall and recent HT/FT,
league context, goal support, data quality, contradictions and market-specific
thresholds. Venue agreement adds support but cannot override major conflicts.

## 2. Aggressive

The most specific credible expression of the match story. It favors exact HT/FT,
straight results, Over 2.5, GG and team Over 1.5 while penalizing broad
protection markets.

## 3. Safer

The protected version of the primary story. It can use DNB, Double Chance,
Over 1.5, Under 3.5 or team Over 0.5. It should remain aligned with the primary
direction rather than inventing a separate match story.

## 4. Venue Pattern

The Potosi-style engine. It reads the home venue profile against the away
venue's opposite profile across all nine transitions. It collapses the leading
routes into one practical market. For example, 1/1 plus X/1 points to Home Win,
while 2/2 plus X/2 points to Away Win.

Papa Primary remains the website default.
