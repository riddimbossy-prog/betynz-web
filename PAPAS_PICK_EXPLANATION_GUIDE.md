# Papa's Pick Explanation Format

Every explanation follows this order:

1. **Papa's Pick:** the practical selected market.
2. The home team's leading venue HT/FT occurrence and sample.
3. The away team's opposite HT/FT occurrence and sample.
4. The strongest exact transition.
5. The next compatible route.
6. Why the practical market covers the important routes.
7. Why Double Chance was unnecessary, necessary, or rejected.

The explanation is generated from the individual fixture profiles and is not a
generic paragraph copied across matches.
