# Betynz Rebel Engines — Goal-Market Rules v1.1

These rules replace every earlier Over/Under range.

## Over 2.5

Requirements:

- Over 2.5 odds: **1.20–1.70**.
- Under 3.5 odds: **greater than 1.60**.
- Over 2.5 is stable or shortening.
- Under 3.5 is stable or drifting and does not shorten more strongly.
- No major BTTS No or First-Half Under 1.5 contradiction.

Downgrade ladder:

1. Over 2.5
2. Over 2.0 Asian Goals
3. Over 1.5 only at 1.30 or lower
4. No Bet

Reject when Over 2.5 is outside 1.20–1.70 or Under 3.5 is 1.60 or lower.

## Under 2.5

Requirements:

- Under 2.5 odds: **1.20–1.70**.
- Over 1.5 odds: **greater than 1.60**.
- Under 2.5 is stable or shortening.
- Over 2.5 is stable or drifting.
- No strong first-half goal pressure.

Downgrade ladder:

1. Under 2.5
2. Under 3.0 Asian Goals
3. Under 3.5 only at 1.40 or lower
4. No Bet

Reject when Under 2.5 is outside 1.20–1.70 or Over 1.5 is 1.60 or lower.

## Under 3.5

Under 3.5 may be selected only at **1.40 or lower**.

When Under 3.5 is above 1.40:

1. Reject Under 3.5.
2. Check Over 1.5.
3. Select Over 1.5 only at **1.30 or lower**.
4. Otherwise return No Bet.

## Over 3.5

Requirements:

- Over 3.5 odds: at least 1.20 and **below 1.90**.
- Over 2.5 odds: 1.20–1.70.
- Under 3.5 odds: greater than 1.60.
- First-Half Over 1.5 and either BTTS Yes or Team Over 1.5 confirm.

Downgrade ladder:

1. Over 3.5
2. Over 2.5
3. Over 1.5 only at 1.30 or lower
4. No Bet

## Over 1.5

Over 1.5 is the main safety downgrade and must be **1.30 or lower**.

It may replace:

- rejected Under 3.5 above 1.40;
- failed Over 2.5 with positive direction;
- failed Over 3.5 at 1.90 or higher;
- a general goal signal that cannot support an aggressive line.

## Movement standards

Spartacus:

- at least 4% main-market movement;
- at least 55% bookmaker agreement;
- at least one related confirmation;
- one mild contradiction may cause a downgrade.

Leonidas:

- at least 6% main-market movement;
- 8% preferred;
- at least 70% bookmaker agreement;
- at least two related confirmations;
- no major contradiction.
