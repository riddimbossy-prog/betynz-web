# Betynz.com — Rebel Engines Goal-Market Correction v1.1

This document replaces every earlier Over/Under 1.5, 2.5 and 3.5 rule for Spartacus and Leonidas.

## 1. Over 2.5 Goals

Required:

- Over 2.5 odds: **1.20–1.70**.
- Under 3.5 odds: **greater than 1.60**.
- Over 2.5 shortens.
- Under 3.5 remains stable or drifts.
- Over 1.5 remains strongly supported.
- No combined strong BTTS No and First-Half Under 1.5 contradiction.

The Under 3.5 price above 1.60 is mandatory.

Ladder:

1. Strong: Over 2.5.
2. Medium: Over 2.0 Asian Goals.
3. Final safety: Over 1.5 only at 1.30 or lower.
4. No Bet.

Reject Over 2.5 outside 1.20–1.70, when Under 3.5 is 1.60 or lower, when Under 3.5 shortens more strongly, or when BTTS No and First-Half Under 1.5 both shorten strongly.

## 2. Under 2.5 Goals

Required:

- Under 2.5 odds: **1.20–1.70**.
- Over 1.5 odds: **greater than 1.60**.
- Under 2.5 shortens.
- Over 2.5 remains stable or drifts.
- Under 3.5 remains supported.
- No strong first-half goal pressure.

The Over 1.5 price above 1.60 is mandatory.

Ladder:

1. Strong: Under 2.5.
2. Medium: Under 3.0 Asian Goals.
3. Final safety: Under 3.5 only at 1.40 or lower.
4. No Bet.

When Over 1.5 is 1.30 or lower, the invalid Under 2.5 signal is rejected and Over 1.5 is checked as a replacement.

## 3. Under 3.5 Goals

Required:

- Under 3.5 odds: **1.40 or lower**.
- Under 3.5 shortens or is strongly stable across the required bookmakers.
- Over 2.5 does not shorten violently.
- First-Half Over 1.5 is not strongly supported.
- No Team Over 2.5 market receives major support.

Automatic switch:

- Under 3.5 above 1.40 is rejected.
- Check Over 1.5.
- Over 1.5 may replace it only at 1.30 or lower, while stable/shortening, without a strong Under 2.5 or low-scoring HT/FT contradiction, and with a related goal confirmation.
- Otherwise: No Bet.

## 4. Over 3.5 Goals

Required:

- Over 3.5 odds: **1.20–1.89**.
- Over 3.5 shortens.
- Over 2.5 odds: **1.20–1.70**.
- Under 3.5 odds: **greater than 1.60**.
- First-Half Over 1.5 confirms.
- BTTS Yes or Favourite Team Over 1.5 confirms.
- No strong Under 3.5 movement.

Ladder:

1. Strong: Over 3.5.
2. Medium: Over 2.5 when its exact range and Under 3.5 confirmation remain valid.
3. Final safety: Over 1.5 only at 1.30 or lower.
4. No Bet.

Any Over 3.5 price of 1.90 or higher is rejected.

## 5. Over 1.5 Goals

Over 1.5 is the final positive-goals safety market.

Required:

- Odds: **1.30 or lower**.
- Stable or shortening.
- No valid strong Under 2.5 signal.
- No dominant low-scoring HT/FT pattern.
- At least one of: Over 2.5 shortens; First-Half Over 0.5 shortens; Favourite Team Over 1.5 shortens; BTTS Yes shortens; Under 3.5 drifts above 1.40.

It is checked after a rejected Under 3.5, a reduced Over 2.5, a rejected Over 3.5, or a general positive-goal signal that cannot support the aggressive line.

Over 1.5 above 1.30 is always No Bet.

## 6. Spartacus movement standard

- Minimum main-market movement: **4%**.
- Minimum bookmaker agreement: **55%**.
- At least one related-market confirmation.
- One mild contradiction may be absorbed only by a downgrade.
- Outputs: primary, one-level downgrade, two-level downgrade, or No Bet.

## 7. Leonidas movement standard

- Minimum main-market movement: **6%**.
- Preferred main-market movement: **8% or higher**.
- Minimum bookmaker agreement: **70%**.
- At least two related-market confirmations.
- No major contradiction.
- First-half or HT/FT confirmation is used when available.
- Outputs: primary, one protected downgrade, one final safety downgrade, or No Bet.

## 8. Decision order

Evaluate:

1. Over 3.5.
2. Over 2.5.
3. Under 2.5.
4. Under 3.5.
5. Over 1.5.
6. No Bet.

This order is not automatic priority. Every candidate must pass its exact price, movement, agreement, confirmation and contradiction rules. The strongest valid candidate wins; order is only a tie-breaker.

## 9. Required examples

- Over 2.5 at 1.58 with Under 3.5 at 1.68 and positive goal confirmation: Over 2.5.
- Over 2.5 at 1.62 with Under 3.5 at 1.52: reject Over 2.5; check Over 1.5 at 1.30 or lower.
- Under 2.5 at 1.64 with Over 1.5 at 1.72 and slow-goal confirmation: Under 2.5.
- Under 2.5 at 1.55 with Over 1.5 at 1.28: reject Under 2.5; check Over 1.5.
- Under 3.5 at 1.48 with Over 1.5 at 1.27: switch to Over 1.5.
- Under 3.5 at 1.46 with Over 1.5 at 1.38: No Bet.
- Over 3.5 at 1.84 with Over 2.5 at 1.55, Under 3.5 at 1.72 and first-half support: Over 3.5.
- Over 3.5 at 1.96 with Over 2.5 at 1.64 and Under 3.5 at 1.67: reject Over 3.5; downgrade to Over 2.5.
