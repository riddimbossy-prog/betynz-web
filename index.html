TRANSPARENT MODEL

How Betynz Works

Every selection must pass data-quality, specialist and Zeus consensus controls.

01

Data Quality Gate

Fixture identity, team samples, venue samples, league history and current odds are checked before any engine can publish.

02

17 Specialist Engines

Fifteen Olympian specialists assess control, trends, goals, form and value. Leonidas and Spartacus independently test opening-to-current bookmaker movement.

03

Zeus Consensus

Opposite markets, weak leads and low-confidence agreement are rejected. No match is forced onto the board.

04

Pre-Kickoff Lock

Qualified selections lock inside the 12-hour window. Only those locked records count toward public performance.

Public grading

A1 requires at least three specialist confirmations, a strong consensus lead and an 88+ model score. A2 requires at least two confirmations and an 82+ score. Lower-quality or contradictory matches are not published.

Model scores are not guarantees or real-world probabilities. Football outcomes remain uncertain.
